Corporate Tax

The corporate tax rate drops from 9.8% to 8.4%.
The cost of this proposal is $318,000,000. He also proposes measures to expand the types of businesses that pay this tax, earning and additional $322,900,000. This brings in an additional $5,000,000, to a total of $1,959,000,000.

Cigarette and tobacco tax

Raises state cigarette tax to $1.42 per pack.
The current state cigarette tax is $.48 per pack, plus a $.75 health impact fee. Dayton proposes raising the tax $.94 per pack, to $1.42 per pack. The health impact fee would remain unchanged, so the total tax and fee on a pack of cigarettes would rise to $2.17 from $1.23.
Taxes on tobacco products would raise to 90% of wholesale price.
The tobacco products tax is currently assessed at 35% of the wholesale price. The governor proposes raising it to 55%. The health impact fee of 35% of the price would remain unchanged. The total tax and fee on tobacco products would rise from 70% of the wholesale price to 90%.
Taxes from tobacco products would raise 95%.
The combined impact of these changes is estimated to raised $369,900,000 for the general fund, almost double the $389,000,000 expected under current law.

Other tax changes

Freezing an increase in state property tax for business would cost $25,100,000.
The governor proposes freezing an increase in state property tax for businesses based on inflation. The proposal would cost $25,100,000. Various small adjustments will result in other state taxes bringing in $2,000,000 less than projected.
